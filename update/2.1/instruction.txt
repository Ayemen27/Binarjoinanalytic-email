FILE CHANGES
——————————————————————————————————————
1. Upload Files

Upload the update.zip file to the public_html directory.
Extract the contents of the zip file.


2. Update the .env File

Open the .env file in your root directory.
Add the following line at the end of the file:

MAINTENANCE_MODE="false"
CACHE_STORE=file


*** Important: Ensure there are no extra spaces before or after the value.


LAST STEP
——————————————————————————————————————

Go to:
https://yoursite.com/admin_path/update

Clear the cache and cookies in your browser:
https://yoursite.com/admin_path/settings/cache

Remember to change the admin_path and yoursite.com to your actual site’s details.
